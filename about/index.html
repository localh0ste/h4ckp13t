About

About Panipat Institute Of Engineering and Technology (PIET)

Panipat Institute of Engineering and Technology (PIET) is a premier institute in the field of engineering and technology. Established with a vision to provide quality education and foster research and innovation, PIET offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. The institute is known for its excellent faculty, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a vibrant campus life.

PIET aims to create professionals who can contribute to the technological advancements and meet the challenges of the modern world. With a focus on holistic development, PIET encourages students to participate in various extracurricular activities, including clubs and societies.

About H4CKPI3T Cyber Security Club

H4CKPI3T Cyber Security Club, founded in 2020, is a student-run organization at PIET. Our mission is to provide students with opportunities to enhance their knowledge and skills in the field of cybersecurity. We offer a platform for students to learn, share, and collaborate on various cybersecurity topics.

The club hosts a range of activities, including technical talks, workshops, CTF competitions, and study groups. We aim to create a community of like-minded individuals who are passionate about cybersecurity and ethical hacking.

Weekly meetups every Wednesday @ 6.00pm

What We Do

  • Public speakers - attend lectures from industry professionals
  • CTF competitions - showcase your hacking skills
  • Workshops - learn and practice new cybersecurity skills
  • Study groups - support for both cybersecurity students and enthusiasts
  • Public events - organize trips to major cybersecurity events and conferences
